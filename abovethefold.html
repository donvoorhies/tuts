"Above The Fold" - how to fix, IMHO...

1st step: "Critical CSS"

Source(s):
  • https://medium.com/@katieharron/critical-css-finder-javascript-snippet-and-bookmarklet-84cc8a09596a
  • https://jonassebastianohlsson.com/criticalpathcssgenerator/.
  • https://varvy.com/pagespeed/render-blocking-css.html
Either read Katie Harron's article regarding this topic, and and follow her instructions to generate a bookmarklet, from the javascript which she supplies in her article, to find your page's critical CSS. Or use Jonas S. Ohlson's online tool to find your page's critical CSS
In both cases, insert the "generated critical CSS" as inline CSS between the <head></head>-element of your page.
For more info, read Patrick Sexton's article: "Eliminate render blocking css".

2nd step: LoadCSS

Source:
  • https://github.com/filamentgroup/loadCSS
Do NOT delete your initial, created CSS-file(s) after completing "Step 1", as you'll be using it (or them) in the following!
...</footer>
<script src="//cdn.rawgit.com/filamentgroup/loadCSS/master/src/loadCSS.js"</script>
<script>loadCSS( "path/to/mystylesheet.css" );</script>

Repeat the function-call above, if you have any other CSS-files that you need to use by putting in the following after the first function-call - i.e.:
<script>loadCSS( "path/to/mystylesheet.css" );</script>
<script>loadCSS( "path/to/second_stylesheet.css" );</script>
<script>loadCSS( "path/to/third_stylesheet.css" );</script>
(All other Javascript-/jQuery-stuff follows by going in here...) </body>

Source:

...</footer>
